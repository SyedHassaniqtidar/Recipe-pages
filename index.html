Which biryani is most famous?

Hyderabadi Biryani
One of the most aromatic biryani, the Hyderabadi biryani is a favourite of many food lovers throughout India and the world even.

What are the two types of biryani?


Traditionally, Biryanis can be classified under two categories - Pakki, wherein cooked meat is coupled with half-cooked rice and further cooked - and Katchi which involves raw mutton pieces, marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked together with uncooked rice.

What makes biryani so tasty?


The rice gets its robust flavor from a variety of spices, including ginger, garlic, nutmeg, cardamom, cinnamon, saffron and rosewater, as well as from the meat itself.
