INTRODUCTION BRIYANI

biryani recipe

ingredients

  1. Chicken with Bones ½ kg
  2. Cooking Oil 1 cup
  3. National Bombay Biryani Mix ½ packet
  4. National Ginger Garlic Paste 2 tbsp
  5. Mint Leaves 1 bunch
  6. Green Chilli 20
  7. Potatoes Cut into Half 2
  8. Onion Boiled and Pureed 1
  9. Biryani Essence 1 tsp
  10. Yogurt 500 gm
  11. Coriander Leaves 1 bunch
  12. Green Food Color ¼ tsp
  13. Water 3 liter
  14. Lemon Slices 2
  15. National Cumin Seeds 1 tsp
  16. National Salt 2 tbsp
  17. Green Chilies 2-3

    For Rice

  • Rice ½ kg

Directions
  1. To prepare Green Chattni: In a blender, add ½ cup yogurt, ½ cup chopped mint leaves, ½ cup coriander and 5-6 green chillies and blend until till smooth.
  2. Boil rice in water, adding 1 tsp National Salt, lemon slices, National Cumin Seedsand green chilies till 1 kanni.
  3. or the curry, sauté boiled onion paste in oil and don’t let it change its color. Now add 1 tbsp National Ginger Garlic Paste, stir for half a minute. Add chicken, potatoes and ½ pack National Bombay Biryani Mix in it. Cook on low flame till the chicken’s water evaporates.
  4. Put 1 cup water, turn on the lid and cover for 20 minutes. Now evaporate if there is any water. Add ½ cup of whisked yogurt and let it evaporate while stirring.
  5. Add it in the curry with the highest flame on so that the water immediately gets evaporated. Don’t let it turn brown from green.
  6. Assemble them with Biryani and put green food color on the rice. Cook it in steam for 10-15 minutes and serve.
